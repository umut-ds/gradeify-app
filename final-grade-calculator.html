How to Use This Calculator

Enter your current grade, the weight of your final exam, and your desired overall grade. We'll calculate exactly what score you need on the final exam.

Example: If you have an 85% currently, your final is worth 30%, and you want a 90% overall, you'd need a 101.67% on the final (which means it's impossible without extra credit).

Disclaimer: This is an estimation tool. Always confirm with your instructor or syllabus.
