Weighted Grade Calculator

Add all your assignments, quizzes, or exams with their scores and weights. The calculator will compute your overall weighted grade.

How to Use This Calculator

Add each assignment or category (e.g., homework, quizzes, exams) with the score you received and its weight in the course. For best accuracy, the total weight should equal 100%.

Example: Homework (85%, 20% weight), Midterm (78%, 30% weight), Final (90%, 50% weight) → Overall grade: 84.1%

Disclaimer: This is an estimation tool. Always verify your final grade with your instructor or official syllabus.
